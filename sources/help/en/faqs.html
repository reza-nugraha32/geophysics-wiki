Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Below are frequently asked questions about Geophysics Wiki.

+ How to use Geophysics Wiki?

Geophysics Wiki contains courses materials from the geophysical engineering's undergraduate curriculum in Institut Teknologi Bandung, Indonesia. To use Geophysics Wiki, simply search for the course/subject that you want to learn and use the reading materials, problem sets, or the past papers available.

+ Why Geophysics Wiki was created?

Geophysics Wiki came from the idea that an online learning resources carefully crafted and developed could massively benefit anyone studying to become a geophysicist anywhere in the world. Through this initiative, we hope to inspire a new generation of excellent geophysicist and aid them in their journey.
Note that Geophysics Wiki can't be a substitute for a textbook. Please use a proper textbook as the main reference and Geophysics Wiki as supplemental reference.

+ When will the contents be updated?

Currently, Geophysics Wiki only contains courses materials from the geophysical engineering's undergraduate curriculum in Institut Teknologi Bandung, Indonesia. However, if you interested in adding to the content outside those, don't hesitate to contact us and join in developing Geophysics Wiki.

+ Found an error in the content?

Geophysics Wiki is very open to critics and suggestions in order to improve the quality of its contents. Thus, don't hesitate to contact us if you found any error in Geophysics Wiki's contents.
