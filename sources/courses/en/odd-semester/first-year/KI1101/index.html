KI1101

Chemistry IA

Course Description

This course is the first part of university elementary physics course for science and engineering. It covers mechanics, mechanical waves, fluids, and thermophysics.

Prerequisites

None

References

Main Reference(s)
Halliday, D., Resnick, R., & Walker, J. (2014). Principle of Physics, 10th Edition. John Wiley & Sons.
Additional Reference(s)
Serway, R.A. (1996). Physics for Scientist and Engineers, 7th Edition. Sander College.
