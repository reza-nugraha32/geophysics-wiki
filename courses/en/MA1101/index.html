Course Description

This course is the first part of university calculus course for science and engineering. It covers real number system, inequalities, functions and limits, derivatives, applications of derivatives, integrals, applications of integrals, and transcendental functions.

Prerequisites

Precalculus and Algebra

References

Main Reference(s)
Varberg, D., Purcell, E., & Rigdon, S. (2006). Calculus with Differential Equations, 9th Edition. Pearson Education.
Additional Reference(s)
Stewart, J. (2015). Calculus, 8th Edition. Cengage Learning.
