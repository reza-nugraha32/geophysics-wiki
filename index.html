What is OpenGeophy?

OpenGeophy is an online learning resources aiming to aid students around the world who's interested in studying geophysics at university. It is based on a four year undergraduate geophysical engineering's curriculum at Institut Teknologi Bandung, Indonesia.

You can find materials, problem sets, and past papers here.

Our Mission

OpenGeophy came from the idea that an online learning resources carefully crafted and developed could massively benefit anyone studying to become a geophysicist anywhere in the world. Through this initiative, we hope to inspire a new generation of excellent geophysicist and aid them in their journey.
