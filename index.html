What is OpenGeophy?

OpenGeophy is an online learning resources aiming to aid students around the world who's interested in studying geophysics at university. It is based on undergraduate geophysical engineering's curriculum at Institut Teknologi Bandung, Indonesia. You can find materials, problem sets, and past papers here.
