Geophysics Wiki

Geophysics learning resources.

Geophysics Wiki is an online resources for studying geophysics. It's based on a four-year undergraduate geophysical engineering program from Institut Teknologi Bandung, Indonesia. You can find reading materials, problem sets, and past papers here.
