What is Geophysics Wiki?

Geophysics Wiki is an online resources for studying geophysics. It's based on a four-year undergraduate geophysical engineering program from Institut Teknologi Bandung, Indonesia.

You can find reading materials, problem sets, and past papers here. For starter, here's an equation for the complex Fourier transform, $$\begin{equation} H(f) = {\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}} e^{-i2\pi fa} e^{i2\pi ft} df. \nonumber \end{equation}$$
